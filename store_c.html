Transaction Data of Store C (KPI)

with Tableau

Background

I got this dataset from my mini course when we were talking about how to use Tableau to make data visualization. This dataset contains sales transaction records for Store C in 2018. It consists information about store sales data from both online and offline store. There are 8 questions that we will answer, but I only create 3 KPI indicators for Sales (Total Income), Total products sold, and Total transactions.

Question to be answer :

  1. How many transactions in our e-commerce per month?
  2. What is the total income earned per month?
  3. How many total products are sold in our e-commerce per month?
  4. How many transactions by store type?
  5. What is the total income by store type?
  6. How many total products sold by store type?
  7. What is the distribution of our customers by city?
  8. What is the distribution of our customers by gender?
