Electronic Sales Dashboard

with MyQSL and Power BI

Background

This dataset contains sales transaction records for an electronics company over a one-year period, spanning from September 2023 to September 2024. It consists of 20000 records of transactions with detailed information about customer demographics, product types, and purchase behaviors.

Question to be answer :

  1. What is the current trend in transaction at the electronics company?
  2. What is the top-selling product categories or specific products?
  3. What are the key customer insights and preferences?
  4. What are the key findings from the recent order analysis?

Data Cleaning

We are using Common Table Expressions (CTE) and the ROW_NUMBER function to identify duplicate data, along with the WHERE function to find null or blank data. After conducting our examination, we found no duplicates, nulls, or blanks in our dataset. However, there is one data where the gender value is listed as N/A. Since this is only one row and the nominal value is not large or significant, we have decided to delete this entry.

Exploratory Data Analysis

We are analyzing data using the WHERE function, the GROUP BY function, and aggregate functions to gather insights about our dataset. We then save additional results by creating Views or Stored Procedures.

Data Visualization

The Electronics company is experiencing a positive sales trend, achieving the highest total revenue in January 2023. Smartphones are the top-selling product category, driving the majority of sales.

Our consumer demographic spans ages 20 to 70, with a strong preference for Standard shipping. The most common payment methods are PayPal and Credit cards. Notably, more than 75% of our customers are not enrolled in our Loyalty Program. Customer satisfaction is moderate, with an average rating of out of 5.

The company's efficient order fulfillment is demonstrated by a high percentage of completed orders; however, we still need improvement, as over 30% of our total orders were cancelled.

Recommendation

  • Enhance Customer Loyalty : Revamp the loyalty program with compelling rewards and targeted incentives to drive enrollment.
  • Improve Customer Satisfaction : Prioritize customer support, personalize experiences, and actively seek feedback to address pain points.
  • Optimize Order Fulfillment : Implement advanced inventory management, streamline shipping and logistics, and provide real-time order tracking.
  • Product Strategy : Continuously innovate, offer customization options, and leverage market research to identify emerging trends and opportunities.
