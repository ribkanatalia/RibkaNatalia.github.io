French Bakery Sales

with Google Spreadsheets and Google Slides.

Background

The French Bakery A faced significant challenges last month in managing their product quantities. Each day, they struggled to strike a balance, often producing either too much or too little, leading to wasted resources or unmet customer demand. The inconsistency highlighted the need for better predictive insights to optimize their daily production.

Question to be answer :

  1. How is the trend of transactions at the bakery?
  2. Which are the five most sold products?
  3. Which day of the week is the busiest for the bakery?
  4. Which time of the day is the busiest for the bakery?

Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis

We check for missing data by applying filters and removing duplicate entries. Additionally, we create two new columns: the total_price column, which is calculated by multiplying quantity by unit_price_euro, and the day_and_week_num column, which is generated by combining the CONCATENATE, TEXT, and ISOWEEKNUM formulas.

Next, we explore the data using a PivotTable to address our problem. We then create Charts to visualize the data, making it easier to read and understand.

Recommendation

  • Focus on High-Demand Product : the most sold Bread like baguette traditional should be produce and promoted more as they are more demand with customers.
  • Time Production : produce more bread during weekends and in the hours before lunch.
  • Review several product types : Stop producing bread types that are not popular amongcustomers. Consider selling them seasonally.
