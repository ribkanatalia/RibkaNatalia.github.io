Car Sales Dashboard

with Microsoft Excel

Background

This dataset contains sales transaction records for car dealerships for 3 years period, from 2018 until 2020. It consists of over 2000 records of transactions from various branches of car dealerships from around the world. It includes detailed information about customer demographics, product types, and transaction data.

Question to be answer :

  1. How is the trend of transactions at car dealerships for 3 years?
  2. Which type of product are the most sold products?
  3. Are we achieved 90% order shipped and less than 10% order cancelled?
  4. Which type of dealsize gives us the most profit?

Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis

We begin by identifying missing information through filters and removing duplicates. Next, we analyze the data using a PivotTable to address the issue effectively. Finally, we create charts using a PivotChart for clear visual representation, enhancing understanding of the insights gathered.

Data Visualization

Looking at the "Total Sales per Quarter" chart, we can see that the trend of transactions has been increasing steadily over the 3 years. There was a significant spike in sales in the first quarter of 2019, followed by a slight dip in the second quarter. However, sales have been consistently rising since then.

The "Total Sales per Products each Years" chart reveals that Classic Cars have been the most sold products in each year. They consistently have the highest sales compared to other product categories.

The "Percentage Order Fulfillment" chart shows that Shipped orders constitute a substantial portion, consistently exceeding 90% in each year and Cancelled orders remain below 10% throughout the 3 years.

The “Total Sales per Deal Size” chart shows that Medium deal size generated the highest profit of over 5.9 Millon dollars, followed by Small and Large deals.

Recommendation

  • Leverage Classic Cars' Popularity : Continue to invest in marketing and inventory for classic cars, as they are the clear top sellers.
  • Optimize Medium Dealsizes : Focus on strategies to increase the number of Medium dealsizes, as they contribute significantly to overall profits.
  • Maintain Efficient Order Fulfillment : Continue to prioritize efficient order processing and shipping to uphold the high shipment rate and low cancellation rate.
  • Analyze Seasonal Trends : Investigate if there are specific quarters or months with lower sales to identify potential opportunities for targeted promotions or inventory adjustments.
